NameCard.TypeConstraints and Usage
  Extension 0..1ele-1: All FHIR elements must have a @value or children
ext-1: Must have either extensions or value[x], not both
Definition:
Captures the reason for the current state of the Appointment.


Comments:
This is generally only used for &ldquo;exception&rdquo; statuses such as <code>not-done</code>, <code>suspended</code> or <code>cancelled</code>. The reason for performing the event at all is captured in the <code>reason</code> element, not here.
